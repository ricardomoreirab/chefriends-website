Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Chefriends ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Chefriends mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using Chefriends, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Profile Preferences: Dietary preferences, cooking time preferences, ingredient dislikes, and language settings you provide.
  • Usage Data: Ingredients you enter, recipes you save, shopping lists you create, and interactions within the App.
  • Camera & Photo Access: Photos you take or select for ingredient scanning. These images are processed to identify ingredients and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app compatibility and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Chefriends cooking experience.
  • Personalize recipe suggestions based on your ingredients and preferences.
  • Save your recipes, shopping lists, and preferences to your account.
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • Improve and optimize the App's performance and user experience.
  • Communicate with you about updates or respond to support requests.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API: For generating recipe suggestions. Ingredient data and preferences are sent to OpenAI for processing. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Advertising & Tracking

Chefriends uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to serve personalized advertisements. On iOS, we respect your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) preferences. If you opt out of tracking through the iOS prompt, we will not track your activity across other apps and websites for advertising purposes.

You can also adjust ad personalization settings on your device under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your account data and preferences are stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures. We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update or correct your profile information within the App.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at hello@chefriendsapp.com.
  • Opt out of ad personalization through your device settings.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To request data deletion, email us at hello@chefriendsapp.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Chefriends is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

hello@chefriendsapp.com
