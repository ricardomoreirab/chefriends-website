Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Chefriends ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Chefriends mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Profile Preferences: Dietary preferences, time preferences, ingredient dislikes, and language settings you provide.
  • Usage Data: Ingredients you enter, recipes you save, and shopping lists you create.
  • Camera & Photo Access: Photos you take or select for ingredient scanning. These images are processed to identify ingredients and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app compatibility and advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the Chefriends cooking experience.
  • To generate recipe suggestions based on your ingredients and preferences.
  • To save your recipes and shopping lists.
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob.
  • To improve our app and services.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API: For generating recipe suggestions. Ingredient data is sent to OpenAI for processing. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely in our database. Recipes, shopping lists, and preferences are associated with your account. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update or delete your profile information within the app.
  • Delete your account by contacting us.
  • Opt out of ad personalization through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Chefriends is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@chefriendsapp.com.
