Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chefriends?
Chefriends is a cooking app powered by AI. It features 8 AI chef friends, each specialized in a different cuisine (Italian, Japanese, Mexican, Indian, French, Thai, Brazilian, and Spanish). You add your available ingredients, pick a chef, and get personalized recipes with step-by-step cooking guidance.
How does ingredient scanning work?
Simply take a photo of your fridge, pantry, or individual ingredients using your phone's camera. Our AI instantly identifies the items and adds them to your ingredient list. You can also upload a photo from your gallery or type ingredients manually.
Can I scan a dish instead of ingredients?
Yes! You can snap a photo of a dish you'd like to recreate, or type the name of a dish. Chefriends will recognize the dish and generate a recipe for it, adapted by your chosen chef's culinary style.
What are the AI chefs?
The 8 AI chefs are virtual cooking companions, each an expert in a specific cuisine. They have their own personality, cooking style, and signature tips. Choose the chef that matches your craving, and they'll guide you through the entire cooking process with authentic techniques and personal advice.
How do timers and mise en place work?
Each recipe step includes built-in timers you can start with a tap, so you never overcook or undercook. The mise en place checklist at the beginning helps you prepare and measure all ingredients before you start cooking, just like a professional kitchen.
Does Chefriends support drinks?
Yes! Switch to drinks mode and your chef friends will suggest cocktail and mocktail recipes — with or without alcohol. The same step-by-step guidance applies to mixing drinks.
How do I save favorite recipes?
Tap the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your favorites. You can access all your saved recipes anytime from your profile. Your favorites sync across your account.
What is Quick Decide?
Quick Decide is a feature for when you can't choose what to cook. It picks a recipe for you based on your available ingredients and preferences, so you can skip the decision-making and start cooking right away.
Can I customize recipes for my diet?
Absolutely. In your profile, you can set dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.), cooking time limits, available kitchen appliances, and ingredient dislikes. Every recipe suggestion is tailored to your settings.
How do Smart Shopping Lists work?
When a recipe requires ingredients you don't have, they're automatically added to a smart shopping list. The list is organized by store section (produce, dairy, etc.) with precise quantities, so you can take it straight to the store.
Is Chefriends free?
Chefriends is free to download and use. The app is supported by ads displayed via Google AdMob. We may introduce optional premium features in the future.
What platforms is Chefriends available on?
Chefriends is launching first on iOS. Android support is planned for a future release. Join the waitlist to be notified when the app becomes available.
How do I delete my account or data?
You can request account and data deletion by emailing us at hello@chefriendsapp.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
